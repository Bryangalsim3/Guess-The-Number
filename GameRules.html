Game Rules

  • Objective: Guess the correct number to win a prize.
  • Range: The number is between 1 and 100.
  • Attempts: You have up to five guesses.
  • Prizes:
    • First try: P500
    • Second try: P100
    • Third try: P50
    • Fourth try: P20
    • Fifth try: P10
  • End of Game: The game ends as soon as you guess correctly, and you win the corresponding prize.
  • No Repeats: You cannot guess the same number more than once.
  • Exact Guess: The number must be guessed exactly.
